Pack
Walking boots or sturdy shoesArthur's Seat, Fairy Pools, the Quiraing — all require proper footwear. Trainers will slip on wet Highland paths.
Pack
Midge repellent — Smidge or Avon Skin So SoftEssential June–August in the Highlands and Outer Hebrides. Hard to find remotely — buy before you leave.
Pack — essential
Warm mid-layer — fleece for each personCold on summits, ferry decks, and exposed viewpoints even in July.
Pack
Camera + extra memory cards, chargersNo photography shops in remote areas. Bring spares.
Pack
Snacks + coffee flask for overnight drivesServices can be closed between midnight and 5am on the overnight legs.
Pack
Sunscreen SPF 30+ and small first aid kitBeaches on Harris are very exposed. Pharmacies are non-existent on the islands — bring any medication you need.
